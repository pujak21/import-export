About Turmexa Organic

Turmexa Organic is a premium wellness brand by Kailash Overseas, dedicated to exporting India’s finest organic superfoods to global markets.

We specialize in high-curcumin Lakadong Turmeric and nutrient-rich Moringa, carefully sourced from trusted Indian farms. Our products are naturally processed, lab-tested, and prepared to meet international export standards.

With a strong focus on purity, traceability, and bulk export reliability, we support distributors, wholesalers, and private-label brands worldwide.
